Alakh Pandey is not any politicians, not even cracked IIT or UPSC exam. He is not the part of any renowned institution. He is successful Physics Teacher in India. His unique teaching style made him a successful Entrepreneur. In 2019, He launched his batch on his app which is now avalaible on play store. Then after he launched multiple batches online at cheapest price. For this he hired multiple teachers across courtry. Physicswallah has now 1Cr+ downloads.
